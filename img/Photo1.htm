Turkish Cuisine Class in Istanbul

AllTourstoTurkey has prepared a gourmet program for you! This is the perfect opportunity to participate in Turkish cooking classes Istanbul, to discover the specialties as well as the tips of the local cooks. Share an exceptional moment in a kitchen at the locals' home.

For WhatsApp Reservation +90 532 453 03 63
Make a Reservation by Calling +90 532 453 03 63

Program of the day Turkish cooking course Istanbul:

  • Meet at the Spice Bazaar in Eminönü with your guide for this culinary course
  • Explanations by your guide of the effectiveness of spices, their origin, their culinary role as well as their benefits and actions on our body
  • You will then cross over to the Asian continent by boat, accompanied by your guide, to a private apartment for your Turkish cooking class. It is a residential area
  • You will be served with good white wine during your Istanbul Turkish cooking class with our collaborator in order to enjoy your class as best as possible!
  • Share a unique moment preparing a good local meal under the supervision of an outstanding Turkish cook
  • Then, savor what you have cooked, accompanied by a good red wine!
  • Return to the center of Istanbul

The price:

From 2 people: 100 € per person

Pros:

  • The order of the visits can be changed as you wish
  • Half-day program: 9:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. to give you time to visit other places in Istanbul
  • Guaranteed departure from 2 people
  • Payment on the spot
  • The private English-speaking guide will help you skip the line by purchasing skip-the-line tickets
  • Take the boat to understand everyday life and avoid traffic. Unique experience!
  • Includes lunch, ingredients, wine
  • Understandable explanations
    Activity is suitable for everyone! Children are welcome.
